    • Fish are the Best Pet

    When one thinks of an iconic pet, an image of a dog or maybe a cat would pop into the mind of most.
    The phrase "dogs are a man's best friend,"may ring true for many. But today I oppose that belief.
    And am here to tell you; why the Fish, is the best pet.

    Reasons to Believe

    Fish are the best pet for several reasons.

    They come in many different colors, sizes, shapes, etc. to fit every person's needs and desires.
    They are also generally easier to maintain and take care of. They will not make a mess in your home,
    or be loud and annoying. And some variety of fish can even perform tricks.
